Nerite Protocol

Learn to use the Nerite Protocol for innovative loans using staked Ethereum (LSTs) as collateral. Earn yields on these stablecoins and extra rewards from LST loan liquidations, all safely and efficiently.

Synthetix Protocol

Synthetix, a synthetic assets platform that now offers yield on USD stablecoins and SNX, plus a perpetuals exchange and simple leveraged tokens. Trade crypto derivatives, taking advantage of passive income opportunities in the SNX ecosystem.

TrueMarkets

Discover TrueMarkets, a decentralized prediction market platform where you can bet on predictions of various events, such as elections or sports, with total on-chain transparency. Trade futures and options, master hedging and speculation, and stake the $TRUE token.

OverTime

A decentralized sports betting platform that allows betting on various sports, such as soccer and basketball, transparently via blockchain. Besides betting, you can "be the house" by providing liquidity. OverTime innovates by offering total control and potential profits, redefining the sports betting market.

Uniswap

Explore the power of the world's largest decentralized exchange. Learn to provide liquidity, create trading pairs, and generate yields with trading fees. Compare profit opportunities and sell assets efficiently, understanding Uniswap's automated market making mechanisms in its various versions.

MetaMask

Master the most popular wallet in the Ethereum ecosystem. Learn to set up securely, manage multiple accounts, connect to different networks, and interact with dApps. Discover advanced features like integrated swap, gas estimates, and how to protect your assets from scams and phishing.

Kerberus Sentinel3

Protect your assets with Kerberus Sentinel3. Learn to configure this extension, activate real-time protection, and use advanced security features. Master integration with DeFi while keeping your funds secure online, plus explore protected connections with dApps via MetaMask.

Aave

Discover how to use Aave for decentralized loans and deposits, maximizing your yields. Learn to navigate variable and fixed-rate markets, understand liquidation risks, and explore advanced strategies like flash loans and safe leverage on major blockchains.
